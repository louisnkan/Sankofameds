The Sankofa Mission

SankofaMeds was founded on a singular, urgent mission: to reclaim the scientific validity of Africa’s vast botanical heritage.

For too long, the profound ancestral knowledge held by our elders—the roots, barks, and leaves that have healed generations—has been dismissed by formal healthcare systems as mere folklore. We believe this is a critical oversight.

The Akan concept of Sankofa teaches us that "it is not taboo to fetch what is at risk of being left behind." This project serves as a digital bridge, rigorously cross-referencing traditional healing practices with modern clinical research and pharmacological evidence.

"Our goal is to preserve this fading knowledge before it disappears with our elders, validate it for the global medical community, and empower a new generation to appreciate the profound scientific richness of our roots."

— Louis Nkan, Founder
